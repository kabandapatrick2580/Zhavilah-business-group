Common Questions About Our Services

1. What services does ZHAVILAH Business Group offer?

We provide a wide range of business advisory services including:

  • Accounting Services
  • Bookkeeping Services
  • Taxation Services
  • Auditing Services
  • Business Advisory Services
  • Customs Clearing and Forwarding
  • Bonded Warehousing
  • Transport and Logistics
  • Professional Training Services
2. What tax services do you provide?

Our tax advisory services include:

  • Corporate Income Tax (CIT)
  • Personal Income Tax (PIT)
  • Quarterly Installment Prepayment (IQP)
  • Trading License
  • Value Added Tax (VAT)
  • Payroll Taxes
  • RSSB Contributions
  • Withholding Tax
  • District Taxes

We help minimize your tax liability while ensuring full compliance.

3. What business advisory services do you offer?

Our business registration services include:

  • Company registration with Rwanda Development Board (RDB)
  • Annual return filing
  • Business amendments
  • Business plan preparation
  • Beneficial ownership registration
  • Investment certificate requests
  • Certificate of good standing requests
  • Business closure processes

We handle all paperwork and regulatory requirements for you.

4. What training programs does ZHAVILAH offer?

ZHAVILAH Training Center provides practical training from beginner to advanced levels. Our courses cover:

  • Applied Business Accounting
  • Tax Preparation and Declarations
  • QuickBooks Software
  • Financial Decision Analysis
  • Financial Reporting
  • ...and more

Flexible learning options include:

  • Classroom Sessions
  • Day & Evening Classes
  • Weekend Programs
  • Online Learning
  • One-on-One Sessions

Our training typically spans 4 weeks of hands-on, practical sessions.
